Simple sandwich recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Lettuce

Growing Wheat (for Bread)

  1. Prepare soil in a garden or field.
  2. Plant wheat seeds in early spring.
  3. Water and maintain soil, removing weeds.
  4. Monitor for pests and diseases.
  5. Wait for wheat to mature (~3–4 months).
  6. Harvest wheat using scythe or modern method.
  7. Dry the wheat stalks.
  8. Thresh wheat to separate grains from chaff.
  9. Winnow grains to remove remaining debris.
  10. Mill the wheat into flour (stone or modern mill).

Making Bread

  1. Measure flour.
  2. Dissolve yeast in warm water.
  3. Mix yeast, flour, and salt.
  4. Knead dough until elastic.
  5. Let dough rise (1–2 hours).
  6. Punch down dough to release air.
  7. Shape dough into loaf.
  8. Let dough rise again.
  9. Bake at ~375°F (190°C) until golden.
  10. Cool completely before slicing.

Raising and Processing Chicken

  1. Raise chicken from chick to adult (months).
  2. Feed and care for chicken daily.
  3. Prepare for slaughter (ethical, safe process).
  4. Humanely slaughter the chicken.
  5. Pluck feathers.
  6. Eviscerate and clean the bird.
  7. Roast or cook chicken.
  8. Slice meat for sandwich.

Milking Cow and Making Cheese

  1. Milk the cow daily.
  2. Strain milk to remove debris.
  3. Heat milk to proper temperature.
  4. Add rennet to curdle milk.
  5. Let curds form.
  6. Cut curds and drain whey.
  7. Press curds into molds.
  8. Knead/stretch curds for mozzarella.
  9. Chill cheese in salted water.
  10. Slice cheese for sandwich.

Growing & Pickling Vegetables

  1. Plant vegetables (cucumbers, lettuce, etc.).
  2. Water and maintain garden.
  3. Harvest vegetables at peak ripeness.
  4. Wash and prep vegetables.
  5. Slice cucumbers or other vegetables.
  6. Prepare brine (water, salt, vinegar, sugar).
  7. Pour brine over vegetables.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Making Salt

  1. Travel to salt source (mine or ocean).
  2. Collect salt water or rock salt.
  3. Evaporate water or grind the rock.
